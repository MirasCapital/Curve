About Us

Miras Capital is an independent advisory firm specialising in mergers & acquisitions, capital raisings, and strategic advisory services.

We provide comprehensive advice to businesses across various sectors of the Australilan economy, combining deep industry expertise with senior-level attention to deliver exceptional outcomes.

As an alternative to traditional fee structures, Miras Capital often seeks to take a portion of any fee in equity, aligning our interests with those of our clients and fostering long-term trusted-advisor partnerships.
